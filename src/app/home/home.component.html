Since its inception in 1976, KNS started as a small trading company, dedicated to providing export channels for Taiwanese factories.

Over the years, our consistent experience and positive customer feedback have helped us build a stable clientele and establish robust supply chain partnerships with numerous Taiwanese manufacturers.

In the 1980s, we expanded our operations to North America. By the 1990s, we had extended further into Central and South America, with our supply chain reaching partners in Southeast Asia.

Entering the 2000s, we successfully fulfilled numerous orders for European clients. Today, we have a broad customer base across Asia, the Americas, and Europe, and are well-versed in the trade processes and regulations of these regions.

We offer a one-stop service that encompasses requirement gathering, customization, shipping, and inspection, continually striving to enhance customer satisfaction as our core mission.

It's worth mentioning that our company is poised to celebrate its 50th anniversary on December 25, 2026.
